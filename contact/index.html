About + Contact

Creative Nightly is lovingly crafted in Manchester, England. It is the personal blog of Scotty Vernon, a creative technologist and director of Wildflame Studios.

My name is Scotty Vernon, I’m a developer and creative technologist based in Manchester, England. I run a cutting edge digital prototyping company called Wildflame Studios, and have produced work for clients including BBC Sport, BBC R&D, Diageo, and England cricketer James Anderson

At the end of 2014, I set myself a goal to write more. Creative Nightly is the by-product of that goal. A platform for which to record and share development notes, and share awesome things I’ve discovered in the world of tech. Essentially, Creative Nightly is a log of my crusade to stay on top of the ever expanding / advancing web tech industry.
